Privacy Policy

Roadvera · Last updated: 1 July 2026
Developer: Inna Shevchenko, Austria · gron.support@gmail.com

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Roadvera (“we”, “us”, “our”) collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile app to prepare for driving theory exams.

Roadvera is currently offered primarily to users in Austria. We plan to expand availability within Europe over time. This policy is written for users in the European Economic Area (EEA) and elsewhere, with rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) where applicable.

We collect only what we need to run the app, sync your progress, and manage purchases.

2. Who is responsible for your data?

Data controller: Inna Shevchenko (individual developer), Austria
Email: gron.support@gmail.com

There is no separate registered company name for Roadvera at this time.

3. Information we collect

3.1 Account and authentication

When you create an account or sign in, we may process:

  • Email address (if you register with email/password, or if Google / Apple shares it with us)
  • Firebase user ID (a unique account identifier)
  • Authentication tokens managed by Firebase Authentication
  • Sign-in method (email, Google, or Apple)

You may also use the app in guest mode without signing in. In that case, progress may stay on your device only and is not synced to the cloud.

Sign-in options: email and password, Google Sign-In (Google LLC), and Sign in with Apple (Apple Inc.).

3.2 Learning progress and app data

We store training progress, exam results, theory library progress, streaks, points, mistakes, achievements, and app settings. When signed in, this data is synced to Google Cloud Firestore (Firebase) linked to your account.

3.3 Purchases (Roadvera Pro)

On iOS, Apple In-App Purchase processes payment. We receive purchase confirmation and store Pro status in Firestore. We do not store your full card or bank details. Stripe may process payments on other platforms where enabled.

3.4 Advertising (optional)

For free (non-Pro) users, we may show banner ads through Google AdMob when enabled. Pro users do not see these banners.

3.5 Support

If you email us, we receive your email address and message content.

3.6 Technical data

Firebase and Google Cloud may process limited technical data (device type, OS, app version, IP address) needed to operate the service.

3.7 What we do not do

  • We do not sell your personal data.
  • We do not use precise location for core features.
  • We do not access contacts, photos, or microphone for core functionality.

4. How we use your information

We use data to provide the app, sync progress, manage Pro access, show optional ads to free users, respond to support, maintain security, and comply with law.

GDPR legal bases: contract performance, legitimate interests (security), consent where required, and legal obligation.
